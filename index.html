Task 1 Reference

🔹 Discursive Markers (Связующие слова) – B2 level
Study these markers with their Russian translations. They will help you connect ideas fluently.
English Русский перевод Example / Пример
HoweverОднакоI wanted to help. However, I was too busy.
FurthermoreКроме тогоShe is a great manager. Furthermore, she is very supportive.
NeverthelessТем не менееIt was raining. Nevertheless, we went for a walk.
MoreoverБолее тогоThe product is cheap. Moreover, it is high quality.
On the other handС другой стороныSome prefer office work. On the other hand, others love remote.
ConsequentlyСледовательноSales dropped. Consequently, we changed our strategy.
ThereforeПоэтомуHe studied hard. Therefore, he passed the exam.
For instanceНапримерWe have many options. For instance, we could hire a consultant.
In additionВ дополнениеThe hotel has a pool. In addition, it has a spa.
As a resultВ результатеThe team worked late. As a result, they finished early.
